Courses

Our Flagship Classroom Courses ie., a comprehensive 12 months full-time course / 24 months full-time course & weekend course where we guide the aspirant from scratch and teach them every aspect of the examination.
This course consists of classroom lectures, study materials, and test series. The courses are designed in a way that the students can also score the highest marks in their respective class or board exams.

Our online live classes are extremely student-friendly where students can ask and get their doubts solved just like any offline classes.
In a year two session are started, one in May and another in July.

We provide classroom courses in four design categories:
  1. IIT-JEE
  2. NEET
  3. WBJEE
  4. JEXPO

We conduct sectional test and full test series for JEE (Mains & Advanced) & NEET. Questions are prepared by the team of experts on the line of the standard set by JEE & NEET. Sectional test helps students to ace each topic & full test makes them exam ready.

In these exams, it is more important not to make mistakes. So after every test, we do conduct a discussion class so that the students and the teachers can analyze the mistakes made by each student & clear their path to success.

To make our students develop an edge over other competitors, we provide them with comprensive, exhaustive & updated study materials in lucid language.
